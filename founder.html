Dr. Gaurav Pant

Founder & Director
Connect on LinkedIn

A Visionary in Sports Education

Dr. Gaurav Pant is a distinguished sports educator, entrepreneur, and the driving force behind Sportal Corporate. As the Founder and Director, he has dedicated his career to redefining how sports education is perceived and delivered in India and beyond.

Professional Background

With a Ph.D. in Physical Education specializing in yoga and anxiety management, Dr. Pant bridges the gap between academic research and practical application. Prior to establishing Sportal Corporate in 2013, he served as an Associate Professor at Bharati Vidyapeeth Deemed University, Pune, where he honed his expertise in academic leadership and curriculum design.

Impact & Innovation

Dr. Pant is a prolific author and researcher, having published over 30 research papers and authored multiple books on sports management. He holds patents related to sports wellness innovations, demonstrating his commitment to advancing the field through technology and thought leadership.

"Sports is not just about competition; it's a powerful tool for holistic development. At Sportal Corporate, our mission is to harness this power to build a healthier, more resilient society."
