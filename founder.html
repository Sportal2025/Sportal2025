A Visionary in Sports Education

Dr. Gaurav Pant is a distinguished sports educator, entrepreneur, and the driving force behind Sportal Corporate. His work focuses on redefining sports education through research, innovation, and policy-aligned programs.

Professional Background

Holding a Ph.D. in Physical Education with specialization in yoga and anxiety management, Dr. Pant bridges academic research with real-world application. He previously served as an Associate Professor at Bharati Vidyapeeth Deemed University, Pune.

Impact & Innovation

Author of 30+ research papers and multiple books, Dr. Pant also holds patents in sports wellness innovation, contributing to the evolving intersection of sports, health, and technology.

“Sports is not just competition; it is a catalyst for holistic human development.”
