Our Journey

The Evolution of Sportal

2013

The Beginning

Strengthening Physical Education in Schools
Sportal began as a grassroots initiative by three friends focused on improving the quality, structure, and relevance of Physical Education in Indian schools.

2020

The Turning Point

Leadership & Digital Shift During COVID
Under Dr. Gaurav Pant's leadership, Sportal transitioned into online education—responding to the urgent need for continuity and meaningful learning beyond physical classrooms.

2021

The Global Yoga Initiative

Online Education Without Borders
Organized a landmark online Yoga education program where 24 teachers taught 2,500+ students worldwide, marking our first large-scale international academic impact.

2023

University Certifications

From Courses to Credentials
Collaborated with universities to launch 15+ certification programs, certifying 4,700+ professionals across sports, yoga, fitness, and education.

2024

Innovation & System Building

AI, NEP 2020 & Scalable Models
Expanded into AI-powered sports education, NEP 2020–aligned curricula, and research integration for scalable impact in schools and higher education.

2025

Global Recognition

International Training & Impact
Partnered with 4 universities, trained 250+ students in Indonesia (USU), and awarded “Best Sports Education Company” for excellence.

The Vision Ahead

The Vision Ahead

Building the Future of Sports Education
To create a globally connected, research-driven sports education ecosystem—empowering educators, institutions, and professionals with future-ready skills, meaningful certifications, and real-world impact.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a background in sports to join?

Not necessarily. While a passion for sports helps, our courses are designed to take you from foundational concepts to advanced application, whether you are a fresher or a professional looking to pivot.

Are these certifications recognized globally?

Yes, our programs are developed in collaboration with leading universities and industry bodies, ensuring the curriculum meets global standards for sports education and management.

How does the placement support work?

We offer 100% placement assistance. Our dedicated career cell connects you with our network of 50+ partners, including sports leagues, schools, and corporate wellness firms.

Can I take these courses alongside my job or college?

Absolutely. Many of our programs offer flexible schedules and hybrid learning modules specifically designed for working professionals and full-time students.
