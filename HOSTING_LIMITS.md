### 3. Forms (Lead Gen)
*   **Netlify Forms** (Built-in) has a limit of **100 submissions/month** on the free tier.
*   **Your Solution**: We used **FormSubmit.co** for your contact form.
    *   This bypasses the Netlify limit.
    *   You get email leads directly for free.

### Conclusion
Stick to the **Free Tier**.
You typically only need to upgrade ($19/mo) if:
1.  You get more than **50,000 - 100,000 visitors per month**.
